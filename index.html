Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer certificates?

Yes, all our professional courses come with a recognized certificate of completion upon finishing the final project.

Are classes physical or virtual?

We offer both! Our virtual classes utilize Google Meet, while we have physical hubs in select locations for hands-on training.

How do I make payments?

We accept secure bank transfers and card payments via Paystack. We also support WhatsApp Checkout for faster processing.

What is your return policy for gadgets?

Eligible items can be returned within 7 days if defective. Items must be in original packaging with all accessories.
