Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer certificates?

Yes, all our professional courses (Tech & Digital) come with a recognized certificate of completion upon finishing the final project.

Are classes physical or virtual?

We offer both! Our virtual classes utilize Google Meet and WhatsApp, while we have physical hubs in select locations for hands-on training.

How do I make payments?

We accept secure bank transfers for both courses and store items. For the Mbtech Store, we also support WhatsApp Checkout for faster processing.

Do you offer mentorship after courses?

Yes! All our students get lifetime access to the 'Connect Pool' community for ongoing mentorship, job updates, and networking opportunities.

What is your return policy for gadgets?

Eligible items can be returned within 7 days if defective. Items must be in original packaging with all accessories. Please check our legal section for full details.
